Watermelon Lemonade


Description

This watermelon lemonade looks amazing, and it's super simple to make! It's perfect for a party or a summer barbeque and will be loved by kids and adults alike.


Ingredients (Serves 8)

  • 1 large or 2 small watermelons
  • 250 ml lemon juice (from a bottle or squeeze your own)
  • 50 g golden caster sugar
  • 1 l bottle soda water
  • 1 lime, cut into slices
  • Small handful mint
  • Crushed ice

Steps

  1. Cut the watermelon in half
  2. Using a large spoon, scoop out the insides of the watermelon and remove the seeds
  3. Mash the watermelon flesh through a seive into a bowl
  4. Put the flesh into a blender with the lemon juice and sugar, and whizz until pureed
  5. Stir in the soda water
  6. Heap some ice into the hollowed-out watermelon and fill it with the lemonade mixture
  7. Serve the rest of the mixture into a jug with some lime slices and mint
  8. Prepare a bowl of crushed ice on the side, so people can help themselves
