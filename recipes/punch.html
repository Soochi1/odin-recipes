Tropical Punch



Description

Passionfruit, orange, lemongrass and delicate fizzy bubbles topped with mint makes this mocktail into the perfect drink.


Ingredients (Serves 4)

  • 1/2 cup (125 ml) pineapple juice
  • 1 orange, thinly sliced
  • 4 lemongrass stems
  • 4 x 375 ml cans chilled Lightly Sparkling Passionrfruit
  • Mint sprigs, to serve

Steps

  1. Divide pineapple juice evenly into 12-hole, 2-tsp (10 ml) ice cube trays
  2. Freeze overnight, or until frozen
  3. In 4 glasses, divide the orange slices, pineabble ice cubes and lemongrass
  4. Pour over the Lightly Sparkling Passionfruit
  5. Top with mint to serve
