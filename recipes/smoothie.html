Calpico Strawberry Smoothie



Description

This refreshing summer smoothie uses a base of Calpico, milk and strawberries. Calpico is a Japanese, non-carbonated soft drink made from lactic acid and non-fat dry milk. Its flavour is similar to that of vanilla yoghurt, or ‘Yakult’.

We recommend using fresh strawberries, but frozen will work fine as well.


Ingredients (Serves 3-4)

Here's what you need to get started:

  • 120 ml calpico concentrate
  • 600ml milk
  • 20 strawberries (approx.)

Steps

  1. Put the calpico concentrate, milk and strawberries into a blender and blend until smooth
  2. Pour over ice (ice not necessary if using frozen strawberries)
  3. Serve immediately
